Kenya National Digital Payment Portal

Official Payment for Digital Typist Registration

To complete your registration for the Kenya Digital Typist Program, a mandatory government processing fee of KSh 280 is required. This fee ensures your identity verification and secures your participation in the national digitization initiative.

Rest assured, this fee will be fully refunded with your very first payout, as part of our transparent and secure process.

Payment Details

An STK Push payment request will be sent directly to your registered M-Pesa account. Please ensure your M-Pesa is active and funded.
